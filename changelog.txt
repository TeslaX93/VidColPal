VIDCOLPAL [workname] - Color Palettes
-------------------------------------
Color palettes set from old computers
and video consoles.

Available in ACO, GPL, PAL (JASC),
PAL (RAW), TXT formats for instant
use in Photoshop, GIMP, PaintShopPro,
Paint .NET, IrfanView, etc.

You can download a single palette, or
download zip with all ( < 1 MB).
